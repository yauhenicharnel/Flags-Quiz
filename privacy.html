Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 2026

This Privacy Policy describes how the Flags Quiz app ("we", "our", or "the app") collects, uses, and protects your information.

Information We Collect

We do not collect personal information such as your name, email address, or contacts.

However, we may collect limited non-personal data automatically, including:

  • Device information (e.g., device type, OS version)
  • App usage data (e.g., quiz progress, scores, interactions)
  • Language preferences

Analytics

We use Firebase Analytics (by Google) to collect anonymous usage data. This helps us understand how users interact with the app and improve the experience.

Firebase may collect:

  • Device identifiers
  • Usage events (e.g., quiz start, quiz completion)

For more information, visit:
https://policies.google.com/privacy

In-App Purchases and Subscriptions

Flags Quiz offers optional in-app purchases and auto-renewable subscriptions through the Apple App Store.

Payments are processed by Apple. We do not collect, receive, or store your payment card details, billing address, or Apple ID credentials.

The app may use information provided by Apple StoreKit to determine whether you have an active subscription or premium access. This information is used only to unlock paid features inside the app, such as removing ads, extra lives, hints, revives, or access to premium game modes.

You can manage or cancel your subscriptions in your Apple ID account settings. You can also restore purchases in the app using the Restore Purchase option.

How We Use Data

  • Improve app performance and features
  • Analyze gameplay behavior
  • Fix bugs and issues
  • Provide and restore premium access

Data Sharing

We do not sell or trade your personal data.

Limited data may be processed by third-party service providers, such as Firebase Analytics and Apple StoreKit, only as needed to provide analytics, app functionality, in-app purchases, and subscriptions.

Children's Privacy

This app is suitable for general audiences. We do not knowingly collect personal data from children.

Your Choices

  • You can stop data collection by uninstalling the app
  • You can manage or cancel subscriptions in your Apple ID account settings
