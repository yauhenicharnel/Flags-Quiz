Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 2026

This Privacy Policy describes how the Flags Quiz app ("we", "our", or "the app") collects, uses, and protects your information.

Information We Collect

We do not ask you to provide personal information such as your name, email address, or contacts.

However, the app may collect or process limited data automatically, including:

  • Device information (e.g., device type, operating system version)
  • App usage data (e.g., quiz progress, scores, interactions, quiz starts, and quiz completions)
  • Language preferences
  • Purchase or subscription status needed to provide premium access

Analytics

We use Firebase Analytics (by Google) to collect app usage data. This helps us understand how users interact with the app, improve the experience, fix issues, and evaluate app performance.

Firebase may collect or process:

  • Device identifiers
  • Device and app information
  • Usage events (e.g., quiz start, quiz completion, feature interactions)

For more information, visit:
https://policies.google.com/privacy

In-App Purchases and Subscriptions

Flags Quiz offers optional in-app purchases and auto-renewable subscriptions through the Apple App Store.

Payments are processed by Apple. We do not collect, receive, or store your payment card details, billing address, or Apple ID credentials.

The app may use information provided by Apple StoreKit to determine whether you have an active subscription or premium access. This information is used only to unlock paid features inside the app, such as removing ads, extra lives, hints, revives, or access to premium game modes.

You can manage or cancel your subscriptions in your Apple ID account settings. You can also restore purchases in the app using the Restore Purchase option.

How We Use Data

  • Improve app performance and features
  • Analyze gameplay behavior and feature usage
  • Fix bugs and technical issues
  • Provide and restore premium access
  • Maintain app functionality and user preferences

Data Sharing

We do not sell or trade your personal data.

Limited data may be processed by third-party service providers, such as Firebase Analytics, Google AdMob, and Apple StoreKit, only as needed to provide analytics, advertising, app functionality, in-app purchases, and subscriptions.

These third-party services may process data according to their own privacy policies and applicable laws.

Children's Privacy

This app is suitable for general audiences. We do not knowingly ask children to provide personal information such as name, email address, or contacts.

If you believe that a child has provided personal information to us, please contact us so we can take appropriate action.

Your Choices
